How We Work
01
Phase One
Discovery & Consultation
We begin with an in-depth conversation to understand your vision, goals, budget, and timeline. This foundational session shapes everything that follows and ensures complete alignment before any work begins.
1–2 Weeks
02
Phase Two
Concept Development
Our designers translate your brief into compelling concepts — mood boards, 3D visualizations, and spatial layouts refined through collaborative review and your feedback at every stage.
2–4 Weeks
03
Phase Three
Design & Documentation
Detailed drawings, material specifications, and technical documentation are prepared meticulously — every element refined before construction begins to ensure complete precision.
3–5 Weeks
04
Phase Four
Execution & Delivery
We oversee every aspect of construction and installation, coordinating contractors, managing timelines, and ensuring the final result matches the design vision exactly.
4–12 Weeks
